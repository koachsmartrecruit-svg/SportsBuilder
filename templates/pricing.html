Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The free plan has no time limit. You can build and publish one website at no cost, forever.
Can I change my template later?
Yes. You can switch templates at any time from the site editor. Your content is preserved.
What does "remove branding" mean?
On the free plan, a small "Built with SportsBuilder" badge appears on your site. Pro and Club plans remove it.
